Why quit Video games

Video games is a good way to have fun, but that fun can become unhealthy in quite a few ways. The first way is by causing loss of motivation and not caring of once self. The second way it cause the person to lose interest in other activities. The third reason loss of relationships either friends or romantic ones.

Eliminate bad habits

Any type of bad habit should be eliminated because they don't serve a purpose at the certain point you are at. When you give up those bad habits in general you will be happier for doing that. As a result you will live a happier and fulfilled life in the long run. Remember the challenge is temporary but at the end you will win.

Follow your dreams

Follow what you love don't settle due to your circumstances with bad habits. Don't let your bad habits determine your future. Be the owner of your future and rule over it. Remember bad habits do not rule you, you defeat those bad habits by any means.
